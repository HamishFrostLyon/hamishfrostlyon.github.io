I'm Hamish, a management consultant by trade, writer by accident. "Ogilvy on Advertising" cracked open the door to long-form writing—though my brief dance with copywriting confirmed that Mad Men and management consultants occupy different universes. Still, I discovered something better: writing as alchemy, transforming the raw material of work into something approaching wisdom.

When my laptop shuffled off this mortal coil in mid-2024, GitHub became my digital monastery. The discovery of Pages in July 2025 was pure serendipity—suddenly, these private meditations had a public garden.

What grows here is yours to harvest. Ideas, after all, are meant to travel.
