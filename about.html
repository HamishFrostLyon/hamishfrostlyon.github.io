I'm Hamish, a management consultant. A while back, "Ogilvy on Advertising" reintroduced me to long-form writing. I found the practice of writing reflective and worthwhile, so I've kept it up as a way to process ideas from work. After my laptop died in mid-2025, I thought backing everything up on GitHub was a good idea, and then I discovered Pages.

I'm experimenting with an editorial pipeline inspired by CI/CD practices – automating quality checks and cross-references between pieces. I'm also building a Zettelkasten-style system to create a knowledge graph of interconnected ideas. The underlying philosophy is simple: do good works, and work conquers all. Please excuse any editorial quirks while I refine these systems.

If anything here resonates with you, feel free to use it – though I provide no warranty or guarantee, and assume no liability for how you apply these ideas.
